About

Who are we?

Team 10975 is a relatively new team, but one who is dedicated to learning, applying, and teaching as much as possible. Members learn not only technical skills in engineering, but also learn the fundamentals of running a business such as gaining funding, leadership, project management, marketing, etc. More important than any competitive success or awards, the students of Team 10975 grow academically, intellectually, and socially. Team 10975 members gain a better understanding, a greater respect, and a more profound appreciation of the benefits of a good education.
